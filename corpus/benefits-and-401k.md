# Benefits and Retirement

## Health Insurance

Meridian offers medical, dental, and vision insurance. Coverage becomes
effective on the first day of the month following the employee's start date.
Meridian pays 90 percent of the premium for employees and 70 percent of the
premium for dependents. Employees choose their plan during onboarding or during
the annual open enrollment period in November.

## 401(k) Retirement Plan

Employees may contribute to a 401(k) plan starting on their first day. Meridian
matches 100 percent of employee contributions up to 4 percent of salary, and
the match is immediately vested, meaning it is the employee's money right away
with no waiting period. Employees may enroll or change their contribution at any
time through the benefits portal.

## Enrollment Windows

New employees have 30 days from their start date to enroll in health insurance
and elect benefits. Employees who miss this window must wait until the November
open enrollment period unless they have a qualifying life event, such as
marriage, birth, or loss of other coverage, which opens a 30-day special
enrollment window.

## Additional Benefits

Meridian provides an annual learning and development budget of $1,500 per
employee for courses, books, and conferences. The company also offers a $1,000
annual wellness reimbursement for gym memberships, fitness equipment, or mental
health services, and life insurance equal to one times annual salary at no cost
to the employee.
