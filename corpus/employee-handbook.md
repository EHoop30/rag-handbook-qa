# Employee Handbook Overview

Welcome to Meridian Robotics. This handbook summarizes the policies that
govern day-to-day work at the company. It is a fictional document used to
demonstrate a document question-answering system; any resemblance to a real
company is coincidental.

## About Meridian Robotics

Meridian Robotics designs autonomous inspection drones for industrial sites.
The company was founded in 2016 and is headquartered in Austin, Texas, with
remote employees across the United States. The standard work week is 40 hours.

## Working Hours

Regular business hours are 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM in the employee's local time
zone. Meridian operates on a flexible schedule, so employees may shift their
hours as long as they are reachable during company core hours of 10:00 AM to
3:00 PM Eastern Time and meet their commitments. Non-exempt employees must
record their hours in the timekeeping system.

## Employment Classification

Employees are classified as either exempt (salaried) or non-exempt (hourly).
Non-exempt employees are eligible for overtime pay at 1.5 times their regular
rate for hours worked beyond 40 in a week. Contractors are engaged separately
and are not covered by this handbook.

## Where to Get Help

Questions about pay, benefits, or policy should go to the People team at
people@meridian.example. Technical and security questions go to the IT team
at it@meridian.example. This handbook is reviewed annually and the People team
publishes the current version on the internal wiki.
