# Paid Time Off and Leave

## Vacation (PTO)

Full-time employees accrue paid time off each month. During the first three
years of employment, employees accrue 15 days of PTO per year. Starting in the
fourth year, the annual accrual increases to 20 days. PTO accrues monthly and
unused PTO of up to 5 days may be carried over into the following calendar
year; any balance above 5 days is forfeited on December 31.

To request PTO, submit the request in the HR system at least two weeks in
advance for absences longer than three days. Shorter absences require manager
approval but no fixed notice period.

## Sick Leave

Employees receive 10 days of paid sick leave per calendar year, separate from
PTO. Sick leave does not carry over and is not paid out on termination. A
doctor's note is required for sick absences longer than three consecutive days.

## Parental Leave

Meridian provides 12 weeks of fully paid parental leave to all new parents,
including birth, adoption, and foster placement. Parental leave must be taken
within 12 months of the child's arrival and may be taken in up to two separate
blocks. Employees are eligible after 6 months of employment.

## Company Holidays

Meridian observes 11 paid company holidays each year: New Year's Day, Martin
Luther King Jr. Day, Memorial Day, Juneteenth, Independence Day, Labor Day,
Thanksgiving and the day after, Christmas Eve, Christmas Day, and one floating
holiday the employee may use at any time.

## Bereavement Leave

Employees may take up to 5 days of paid bereavement leave for the death of an
immediate family member and up to 1 day for extended family.
