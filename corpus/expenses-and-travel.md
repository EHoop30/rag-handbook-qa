# Expenses and Travel

## Reimbursable Expenses

Meridian reimburses reasonable business expenses. Common reimbursable items
include software subscriptions used for work, professional development, client
meals, and approved travel. Personal expenses, commuting to a local office,
and alcohol are not reimbursable.

## Approval Thresholds

Expenses under $75 do not require a receipt but should still be itemized in the
expense system. Expenses of $75 or more require an itemized receipt. Any single
expense over $500 requires manager pre-approval before the purchase is made.
Recurring software purchases over $1,000 per year require director approval.

## Submission Deadline

Expenses must be submitted within 30 days of the purchase date. Expenses
submitted more than 60 days after purchase will not be reimbursed except in
documented exceptional circumstances. Reimbursements are paid on the next
payroll cycle after approval.

## Travel

For approved business travel, Meridian pays for airfare (economy for flights
under 5 hours, premium economy allowed for longer flights), lodging, and ground
transportation. Employees receive a per diem of $65 per day for meals and
incidentals on domestic travel and $85 per day for international travel, which
does not require itemized receipts.

## Mileage

Employees who use a personal vehicle for approved business travel are
reimbursed at $0.67 per mile. Mileage is logged in the expense system with the
trip purpose and date. Commuting mileage to a regular work location is not
reimbursable.
