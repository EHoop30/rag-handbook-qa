# Remote Work Policy

## Eligibility

All employees are eligible to work remotely after completing 90 days of
employment. New employees spend their first 90 days in a hybrid arrangement so
they can onboard with their team. Fully remote status requires manager
approval and is available in any U.S. state where Meridian is registered.

## Core Hours and Availability

Remote employees must be online and responsive during company core hours of
10:00 AM to 3:00 PM Eastern Time. Outside core hours, employees manage their
own schedule. Employees are expected to keep their calendar current and to
join scheduled meetings from a quiet location with camera available.

## Equipment and Stipend

Meridian provides every remote employee with a company laptop. In addition,
each remote employee receives a one-time home office stipend of $500 to spend
on a desk, chair, monitor, or other equipment. The stipend is requested
through the expense system and requires an itemized receipt. Employees also
receive a $50 monthly internet reimbursement.

## Security Requirements

Remote employees must connect to internal systems through the company VPN and
must follow the device security policy, including full-disk encryption and
multi-factor authentication. Company data may not be stored on personal
devices. See the Security and Devices policy for details.

## Coworking and Travel

Employees who prefer an office may expense a coworking membership up to $250
per month with manager approval. Remote employees are expected to travel to
Austin for one company-wide onsite per year; travel is arranged and paid by
the company.
